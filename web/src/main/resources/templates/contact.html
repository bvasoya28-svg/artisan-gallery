Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I become an artisan seller?

Go to the Account Center and select "Sell Your Item". After accepting the Artisan Agreement, you can start uploading your masterpieces (up to 5 items).

2. Is there a listing fee?

The first 5 listings are completely free for all verified creators. We want to support the artisan community by keeping our platform accessible.

3. How long does shipping take?

Since each item is handmade, shipping times vary. Generally, items are dispatched within 2-4 days. You can track your order status in the "Order History" section.

4. Can I delete a product once it's uploaded?

Yes, you can delete items from your Seller Dashboard. However, please note that deleting an item does not reset your 5-product limit.

For urgent order issues, please include your Order ID in the email subject line.
