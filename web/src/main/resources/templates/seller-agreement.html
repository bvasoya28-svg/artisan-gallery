Artisan Seller Agreement

Please read our terms of service carefully before listing your items.

1. Product Authenticity

All items must be 100% handmade or uniquely crafted by you. Mass-produced or resale items are strictly prohibited.

2. Service Commission & Fees

To support the gallery's operations, the first 5 product uploads for every artisan are completely free. After reaching this limit, a 5% service commission will be applied to all subsequent sales, which will be transferred to the company to maintain your premium visibility.

3. Slot Policy (Important)

Each seller is granted 5 listing slots. Deleting an item does NOT reset your slot count. For example, if you upload 5 items and delete 1, your usage remains at 5/5. This ensures the platform remains a curated collection of permanent masterpieces rather than a rotating storefront.

4. Stock Management

Artisans are responsible for maintaining accurate stock status. If an item is unavailable, it must be marked as "Out of Stock" immediately through your dashboard. You can toggle this status at any time.

5. Quality Standards

Product images must be high-resolution and accurately represent the item. Descriptions should be honest and detailed.

6. Account Integrity

Deleting your account will result in the permanent cascading deletion of all your listed products and associated data.
