Artisan Onboarding

Welcome to the gallery. Please read and accept our creator terms to start selling.

1. Handmade Commitment

Every item listed in the Artisan Gallery must be 100% handmade, vintage, or a unique craft supply created by you. We strictly prohibit the resale of mass-produced commercial items.

2. Seller Constraints (Lifetime Slots)

Each artisan is limited to a maximum of 5 lifetime listing slots. Focus on showcasing your finest work. Please note that deleting a product does not reset your slot count; once a masterpiece is launched, it occupies one of your 5 available slots permanently (e.g. if you upload 1 item and delete it, your count remains 1/5).

3. Image & Description Standards

Product photographs must be clear, high-resolution, and represent the actual item for sale. Descriptions must be honest, detailing materials used and the story behind the creation.

4. Stock Management

Artisans are responsible for maintaining accurate stock status. If an item is unavailable, it must be marked as "Out of Stock" immediately through your dashboard. You can toggle this status at any time.

5. Ethical Conduct

Artisans must engage with customers professionally and fulfill orders within the promised timeframe. Failure to maintain high service standards may result in removal from the gallery.
